Rice Pudding

A Danish rice pudding with toasted almonds and cherry sauce.

A recipe for a Danish rice pudding typically eaten around Christmas time. It is very rich, eating too much could prove to be deadly!

Ingredient list

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Whole lemon
  • Whole orange
  • Almonds
  • Frozen cherries
  • Pectin
  • Spices (Cardamom, Cinnamon, Nutmeg)
  • Sugar
  • Cream cheese or marscapone
