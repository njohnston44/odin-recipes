Spaghetti

Spaghetti with meat sauce.

This recipe will provide you with the ingredients and steps to make a wonderful and inexpensive meat sauce for your spaghetti.

The sauce can be made ahead of time and portions of it can be reheated in a pan when you are ready to eat!

Ingredients

  • 1kg ground beef
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 3 carrots
  • 6 mushrooms
  • parsley
  • 2 large cans crushed tomatoes
  • 1 small can tomatoe paste
  • 4 tbps balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tbps white sugar
  • 1 tbps dried oregano
  • 150g dried spaghetti or other pasta

Cooking Instructions

  1. Finely chop onion, pepper mushrooms.
  2. Grate carrots with the large sized grater holes.
  3. Saute vegetables on medium-high heat with some olive oil and salt in a large frying pan until softened.
  4. Remove cooked vegetables from the pan and set aside.
  5. Cook ground beef on high heat until water has been boiled off and meat starts to brown.
  6. Push the browned beef to one side of the pan and add in the can of tomatoe paste along with the oregano.
  7. Allow the tomatoe paste to heat up and brown slightly.
  8. Start mixing the tomatoe paste in the with beef and add the cooked vegetables back into the pan.
  9. Once beef and vegetables are thoroughly mixed, add in the cans of crushed tomatoes and mix.
  10. Reduce heat to low, add in a few bay leaves and cover with a lid.
  11. Boil water to cook your pasta.
  12. Once pasta is nearly cooked, store extra sauce in the fridge.
  13. Once pasta is cooked to desired tenderness, drain and add the pasta to the sauce in the pan.
  14. Stir pasta and sauce together and put it on a plate.
  15. Garnish with parsley, freshly cracked black pepper, and crushed red pepper flakes if desired.
