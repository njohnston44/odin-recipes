Rice Pudding

A Danish rice pudding with toasted almonds and cherry sauce.

A recipe for a Danish rice pudding typically eaten around Christmas time. It is very rich, eating too much could prove to be deadly!

Ingredient list

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Whole lemon
  • Whole orange
  • Almonds
  • Frozen cherries
  • Pectin
  • Spices (Cardamom, Cinnamon, Nutmeg)
  • Sugar
  • Cream cheese or marscapone

Cooking Instructions

  1. The night before mix frozen cherries, pectin and sugar in a bowl and place in the fridge.
  2. The next day warm milk in a sauce pan with spices but do not let it boil
  3. On a seperate burner, place another pot or pan and add the rice.
  4. Slowly ladel warm milk over rice and heat, allowing the rice to absorb the milk.
  5. Repeat previous step several times until all the milk has been added to and absorbed by the rice.
  6. Add sugar, orange zest, lemon zest, and cream cheese to the rice.
  7. Place rice in a container and allow to cool.
  8. Toast chopped almonds in the oven until golden brown.
  9. Juice the lemon and orange into the cherry sauce.
  10. Serve by spooning some rice pudding into a bowl and top with cherry sauce and almonds.
